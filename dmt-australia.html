DMT: Australia

Australia

  • Location: Worldwide (Varies)
    Organization: Committee on Data of the International Council for Science - Research Data Alliance (CODATA-RDA)
    Topics: Various; foundational data skills across disciplines
    Format: In-person workshops
    Link: http://www.codata.org/events/training-workshops
  • Location: Across Australia (Adelaide, Brisbane, Canberra, Melbourne, Parramatta, Perth, Sydney)
    Organization: Data Management Association (DAMA)
    Topics: Resources for data management trainings, in-person events, and guides. DAMA also offers a mentoring program which connects individuals to Australia's most senior and prominent data professionals from various industries, as well as teaching and administering the international certification program - Certified Data Mangement Professional (CDMP).
    Format: In-person and virtual trainings; online guides and physical guides for purchase
    Link: https://www.dama.org.au/index.php
