DMT: North America

North America

  • Organization: Collaborative Research Center for American Indian Health (CRCAIH)
    Location: North America
    Topics: Tools and best practices for American Indian Tribal Nations and other Indigenous nations for collecting and storing research data
    Format: Virtual resources
    Link: https://www.crcaih.org/training-and-resources/data-management-toolkit.html
  • Organization: Committee on Data of the International Council for Science - Research Data Alliance (CODATA-RDA)
    Location: Worldwide (Varies)
    Topics: Various; foundational data skills across disciplines
    Format: In-person workshops
    Link: http://www.codata.org/events/training-workshops
  • Organization: Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research (ICPSR) at the University of Michigan
    Location: Michigan, United States of America
    Topics: Quantitative methods in social research
    Format: Yearly in-person workshop
    Link: https://www.icpsr.umich.edu/icpsrweb/content/sumprog/about.html
  • Organization: NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory, California Institute of Technology
    Location: California, United States of America
    Topics: Renewable energy, space exploration
    Format: In-person workshops
    Link: https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/edu/events/2017/1/21/utilizing-renewable-energy/
  • Organization: National Center for Ecological Analysis and Synthesis (NCEAS)
    Location: Various, primarily United States and online
    Topics: Environmental data science
    Format: In-person workshops and online archived trainings
    Link: https://www.nceas.ucsb.edu/training
  • Organization: Portage Network
    Location: Canada
    Topics: Data management training and shared stewardship of research data throughout Canada
    Format: Online training resources and data practices for Canadian researchers
    Link: https://portagenetwork.ca/
