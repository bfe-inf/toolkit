DATA MANAGEMENT TRAINING INVENTORY

For Data Managers

  • Organization: DataONE Education Modules
    Link: https://www.dataone.org/education-modules
    Contains: Lessons on all phases of the research lifecycle involving data. While all of the modules are useful in understanding data management, the following lessons are of particular help to data managers: Lesson 1: Why Data Management; Lesson 3: Data Management Planning; Lesson 6: Protecting Your Data; Lesson 10: Legal and Policy Issues.

  • Organization: MANTRA Research Data Management Training
    Link: https://mantra.edina.ac.uk/informationprofessional.html
    Contains: Data management training specific to professional role; this specific link contains resources for information professionals (including data managers).
